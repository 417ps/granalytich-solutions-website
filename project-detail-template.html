Alternative Water Supply Program

Value: $1.7 Billion Duration: 2019-2024 Location: Colorado Client: Multiple Municipalities

Project Overview

As the Owner's Senior Program Controls Lead, Granalytich Solutions managed one of Colorado's most ambitious water infrastructure programs. This $1.7 billion initiative involved the design and construction of critical water supply infrastructure serving eight municipalities across the region.

The program encompassed multiple complex components including transmission lines, underwater tunnels, pump stations, and storage tanks, all requiring meticulous coordination and control to ensure on-time, on-budget delivery while maintaining the highest standards of quality and safety.

Key Achievements

  • Successfully managed program controls for 8 interconnected municipal projects
  • Implemented integrated master schedule tracking 2,500+ activities
  • Delivered $127M in cost savings through value engineering initiatives
  • Maintained 98% schedule adherence across all work packages
  • Zero safety incidents throughout 5-year program duration

Technical Scope

Transmission Infrastructure

87 miles of large-diameter transmission lines ranging from 48" to 96" in diameter, including complex river crossings and urban installations.

Underwater Tunnels

3.2 miles of underwater tunnel construction using advanced TBM technology, including geological risk management and groundwater control.

Pump Stations

Five major pump stations with combined capacity of 450 MGD, featuring redundant systems and advanced SCADA integration.

Storage Facilities

12 storage tanks with total capacity of 850 million gallons, including seismic design considerations and water quality management systems.

Project Controls Excellence

Granalytich Solutions implemented a comprehensive project controls framework that became the model for large-scale water infrastructure programs:

  • Developed and maintained integrated master schedule using Primavera P6
  • Established earned value management system achieving 0.95 CPI/SPI average
  • Created custom dashboard reporting for executive stakeholders
  • Implemented risk register tracking 200+ items with mitigation strategies
  • Coordinated weekly progress meetings with 30+ contractors and consultants

Stakeholder Management

This program required exceptional stakeholder coordination across multiple jurisdictions, regulatory agencies, and community groups. Our team facilitated over 150 stakeholder meetings, ensuring transparent communication and maintaining strong community support throughout the project lifecycle.
